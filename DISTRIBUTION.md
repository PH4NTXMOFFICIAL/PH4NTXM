# PH4NTXM — Distribution

## Distribution Model

PH4NTXM is distributed as a proprietary commercial operating system.

This repository does not provide public downloads, release artifacts, or
installation media. No automated distribution channels are used.

All builds are generated on demand and delivered directly to customers
through official PH4NTXM channels.

---

## Authenticity & Integrity

Direct delivery is used to ensure build integrity, authenticity, and
consistency.

Any PH4NTXM binaries, images, or installation media obtained outside
of official channels should be considered unofficial and unsupported.

---

## Redistribution

Redistribution, mirroring, or public hosting of PH4NTXM in any form
is not permitted except where explicitly allowed under a separate
written agreement.
